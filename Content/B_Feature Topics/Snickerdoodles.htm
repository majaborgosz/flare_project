Snickerdoodles

This recipe makes 25-30 cookies.

Prep time Oven temperature Cook time
10 min 180ºC 15 min

Ingredients

  • 250 g flour

  • 1/2 tsp nutmeg

  • 3/4 tsp baking powder

  • 1/2 tsp salt

  • 125 g butter

  • 100 g sugar (or less)

  • 1 large egg

  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

  • 2 tbsp sugar mixed with 1 tbsp cinnamon, to coat

Method

  1. Sift flour, nutmeg, baking powder and salt into a bowl, set aside.

  2. Beat butter and sugar together until smooth and fluffy. Add in the egg, add the vanilla, mix again until combined. Stir the previously set aside dry ingredients into the mixture and mix again; the resulting dough should not be sticky.

  3. Form walnut-sized balls from the cookie dough mixture, coat them in cinnamon sugar, place them on a baking sheet previously lined with baking paper, gently flatten with the back of a spoon.

  4. Bake at 180ºC for about 15 minutes or until golden brown.
