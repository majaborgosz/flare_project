Pancakes

This recipe serves 4 people.

Prep time Cook time
10 min 1 min each

Ingredients

  • 300 g flour, sifted

  • 2 large eggs

  • 375 ml milk

  • 75 g butter, melted and cooled

  • 3 tsp baking powder

  • 3 heaping tbsp sugar

  • pinch of salt

Method

  1. Lightly whisk the eggs with a kitchen whisk (they should foam slightly), add the milk, butter and mix again.

  2. Sift flour, baking powder, sugar and salt directly into the mixture. Mix with a kitchen whisk until the ingredients are combined and a smooth batter is formed.

  3. Fry in a dry non-stick pan (you can use a little butter or oil), on both sides, until lightly golden brown. Flip when bubbles appear on the surface of the pancake
